At Southampton Tennis Academy we put the player at the centre of everything we do.


We believe that tennis is a game for life and it's our mission to help you develop as a player and a person on and off the court.


We have outstanding adult and junior programmes tailor-made to individuals of all ages and abilities. All of our staff are trained to provide you with everything you will need to improve your game and take it to the next level whether you are a junior club player or a world-ranked adult. STA Tennis is based upon three core principles which we focus on to bring out the best in your game: Competition, Coaching and Practice. You can learn more about how we implement these below.

Competition


We offer a comprehensive internal competition programme for all of our players, regardless of level. During the course of the year we run a series of team events, fun competitions and rankings tournaments. Our coaches are always on hand to advise and help prepare our players for the rigors of competition.

Coaching


With our team of experienced, full-time coaches you won't have to worry about fixing those technical errors in your game. Our attention to detail is scond to none and we even have an on-site Strength and Conditioning team as well as access to qualified Physiotherapists and Sports Psychiatrists.

Practice


Here at STA, we know the practice court is where the real improvement takes place. We'll expect to see you out on court running down every last ball and fighting for every point. We'll give you weekly drills and challenges to complete in your own sessions. Check out our social media to see the latest ones!
